Yi Jiang / 江漪
about       writing       contact      
Yi Jiang is a writer from Shanghai and Wisconsin.

Her fiction has appeared in Ploughshares, Gulf Coast, and Epoch. She received a BA in Economics and Political Science from the University of Wisconsin-Madison in 2017 and an MFA in Fiction from Vanderbilt University, where she was a Russell G. Hamilton scholar and taught fiction and edited for the Nashville Review. She is currently based in Nashville, Tennessee and is a summer instructor at the Sewanee's Young Writers' Conference. She is working on a first novel and collection of short stories.

Prior to a career shift, she worked as an engineer at a certain healthcare tech company. She occasionally does some web design and welcomes freelance requests.
