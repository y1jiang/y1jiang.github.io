Yi Jiang / 江漪

Yi Jiang is a writer from Shanghai and Wisconsin. Currently based in Nashville, TN.

Her fiction has appeared or is forthcoming in Ploughshares, Gulf Coast and Epoch. She received a BA from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and is a second-year fiction MFA candidate at Vanderbilt University, where she teaches fiction and edits for the Nashville Review.

She believes shengjian (生煎包) to be man's greatest invention. For a few years, she worked at a certain healthcare tech company. She has dabbled in web design.
